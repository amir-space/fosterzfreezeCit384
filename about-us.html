About Us

Welcome to Fosters freeze Burbank

At Fosters freeze Burbank, we pride ourselves on being more than just a place to eat — we’re a community staple. Since opening our doors in 1946, we’ve been serving up delicious meals made with fresh ingredients and a passion for flavor.

Our History

Fosters Freeze has been a California tradition since the mid 1940's. The Fosters story begins in 1946 when George Foster opened the first Fosters Freeze in Inglewood, California. He introduced the soft serve cone and a line of soft serve desserts. The unique product became so popular that he opened a chain of restaurants. As California grew, so did the Fosters concept. Made-to-order hamburgers, fries and other food items were added to the menu. Fosters Freeze truly could be described as California's first fast-food chain!
